Get your personality type using your own social media!

We'll predict your Myers-Briggs Type Indicator based off of your tweets.

Overview

The Myers-Briggs Personality Indicator is one of the most accurate personality tests along with many others in scientific literature.

Details

There are 16 personality types based on four categories that the Myers-Briggs test assigns: introversion vs extroversion, sensing vs intuition, thinking vs feeling, and judging vs perceiving.

Method

This personality predictor uses BERT, a state-of-the-art natural language processing model developed by Google.
